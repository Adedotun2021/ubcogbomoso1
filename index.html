Department In Church

Places to Work For Kindom Expansion

Prayer

The Prayer Unit was established to intercede for the church. We stand in gap in the place of Prayer for all members of the church and the propagation of the gospel.

Mission & Evangelism

The Unit was establishes primarily to preach God's Word, telling people simply and clearly what God says concerning His Son Jesus Christ and what He has done for all.

Education

The Education Unit consists of the Sunday School Department, Discipleship Unit, Reading Class, Drama Department, Library And Academic Unit. The Ministry has been known for teaching, accountability, relationship building, influence and good Christian conduct.

Men Missionary Union

The Department exist to equipped men for life and godliness through preaching, praise, possession and purpose.
2 Peter 1:3

Women Missionary Union

"Its purpose being to promote Christian missions through a programme of mission study, prayer, community missions, stewardship and missionary education of women. The purpose which characterized the organisation of WMU at its beginning still holds today."

Youth Fellowship
The Youth department exist to organize different programs to engage its youth both for their spiritual, mental, academic and physical growth through programmes such as Youth Camp, illuminate, Youth Hangout, Skill Acquisition etc. The membership of the youth department is about 1500. The youth fellowship holds every Thursdays by 6pm.
